Welcome To Springfield!

"Home of the Three eyed fish"

Welcome to Springfield, located somewhere in America...probably. Founded by a group led by Jebediah Springfield
Who, after misinterpreting a passage in the Bible, left Maryland trying to find "New Sodom." After he refused to found a town where
men were free to marry their cousins, half of the group left. The dissenters founded the nearby town of Shelbyville. The two cities remain rivals to this day. Today Springfield is home to the worlds first "Aquacar" factory. Named by Times magazine as "America's worst city" and also called "America's Crud Bucket" by Newsweek magazine.
